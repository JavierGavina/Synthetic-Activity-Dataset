Daily Routines

You can press Enter to Add Activity

Here you have to put unique types of days that users do, from what time to what time the user is in one room or another.

A daily routine is completed when the last time slot (ended at 23:59) is written

Schedule Routines

In this section you can create routines by dragging the routines defined on the left to the corresponding day in the calendar. You can undo a routine selection by double-clicking on the day in the calendar. You also have the option to clear all selections with the Reset button. At the end, you can export the routines to a JSON with the Export to JSON button.
